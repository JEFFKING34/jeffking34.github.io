From Bean to cup, with respect

GreenLeaf was founded in 2022 by two brothers who wanted to bring Transparency back to coffee. We visit farms personally, pay above fair-trade prices, and roast in our neighbourhood micro-roastery.

Our core values

  • Sustainable: Solar-powered roastery, zero waste to landfill.
  • Educational: Free brewing workshops every Saturday.
  • Community:5% of profitsgo to local reforestation projects
